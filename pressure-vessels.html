Pressure Vessels

ASME Section VIII Div. 1 pressure vessels, heat exchangers and tanks under our ASME “U” stamp.

Overview

Coded pressure equipment, fully documented

AMF designs and fabricates pressure vessels and heat exchangers to ASME Section VIII Division 1, holding the ASME “U” designator. Every item is welded under an ISO 3834-2 system with qualified procedures, post-weld heat treated where required, inspected and pressure-tested, and delivered with a complete manufacturing data book.

  • Pressure vessels, drums, separators and accumulators
  • Columns, towers and reactors
  • Heat exchangers (shell & tube)
  • Storage tanks and atmospheric vessels
  • Post-weld heat treatment (PWHT) and pressure testing
  • Carbon, stainless and alloy steel construction
