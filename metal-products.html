Overview

Fabricated metal products, fully finished

AMF fabricates a wide range of metal products — from equipment baseframes and ducting to offshore foundation structures — starting from raw plate and sections through to blasted, painted and marked assemblies ready for site. Our large covered workshop and 50-tonne crane capacity (100-tonne tandem) let us handle heavy, oversized fabrications in a single controlled environment.

  • Equipment baseframes and structures
  • Duct & stack, damper and shelter for large equipment
  • Offshore foundation structures
  • Pipe racks, support structures and equipment supports
  • Platforms, walkways, stairs, handrails and access steel
  • Heavy and oversized modular fabrications
